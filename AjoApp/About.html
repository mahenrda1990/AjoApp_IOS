• Ajoapp is your free, peer-to-peer money saving platform for groups of people (friends, family, colleagues) and small businesses to save towards a targeted goal, by jointly contributing agreed amounts over an agreed period. Each person or small business in the group takes the total payout weekly, bi-weekly or monthly. This rotates till the whole group has had a turn at receiving the total payout.
  • Create a group, Invite friends, family or small businesses, Start saving for a goal today.
  • Use Ajoapp to keep track of, and manage your group savings. Ajoapp neither asks for, nor holds your money.
